About Our Courses

Our SSB training programs are designed by ex-SSB assessors to give you the best chance of success in your SSB interview. We offer comprehensive preparation covering all aspects of the SSB interview process with equal emphasis on psychological tests, GTO tasks, and personal interview.

The courses are structured to progressively build your skills and confidence, starting with fundamental concepts and moving to advanced techniques used by successful candidates. You will receive personalized feedback at every stage to help you identify and improve your weak areas.


Who Should Attend

  • First-time SSB aspirants who want a thorough preparation
  • Candidates who have been screened out previously and want to improve
  • Those who want to develop officer-like qualities beyond just SSB preparation
  • Aspiring candidates for all defence services - Army, Navy, Air Force and Coast Guard

What You Will Learn

  • Master psychological tests (TAT, WAT, SRT, SD) with proven techniques
  • Excel in all GTO tasks including GD, GPE, PGT, HGT, GOR, IO, Lecturette, CT, and FGT.
  • Develop a winning strategy for your personal interview
  • Understand what assessors look for in candidates
  • Build confidence through realistic mock tests and feedback

Course Curriculum

Our structured curriculum covers all aspects of the SSB interview process:

Week 1 : Psychology Training

  • Understanding SSB process and expectations
  • PIQ questionnaire practice
  • Officer Like Qualities (OLQs) and how to demonstrate them
  • Thematic Apperception Test (TAT) techniques
  • Association Test (WAT) strategies
  • Situation Reaction Test (SRT) mastery
  • Self Description techniques
  • Psychological profiling and analysis
  • Practice tests with expert evaluation

Week 2: Group Testing Officer Training

  • Group Discussion strategies
  • Group Planning Exercise techniques
  • Progressive Group Task fundamentals
  • Group Obstacle Race preparation
  • Half Group Task strategies
  • Lecturette preparation and delivery
  • Command Task techniques
  • Individual Obstacle demonstration
  • Final Group Task strategies

Week 3: Personal Interview Training

  • Interview preparation and common questions
  • Daily 2 mock interviews with recommended students
  • Current affairs
  • Body language and communication skills
  • Personal story development
  • Mock interviews with feedback

Week 4: Evaluation and Feedback

  • Full mock SSB interviews
  • Performance analysis and improvement plan
  • Final tips and strategies
  • Q&A with experienced assessor
