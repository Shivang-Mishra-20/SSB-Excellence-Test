About SSB EXCELLENCE

At SSB EXCELLENCE candidates learn to understand the evaluation methods and techniques of the Psychologist, GTO and Interviewing Ofiicer. After rigorous practice of over 60+hrs and hundreds of exercises, candidates become so familiar with the TAT, WAT, SRT, SD, GD, GPE, PGT, HGT, CT, IO, GOR, Lecturette, FGT and mock interviews that actual SSB becomes a breeze. Candidates are equipped with a stencil template which allows their thoughts to be presented within minutes as expectd at the SSB. Instead of producing prefabricated spoon fed responses candidates retain their individual flavour and thought process while impressing the psychologist , GTO and IO, guaranteeing a recommendation.

Candidates are personally evaluated on a daily basis with regular custom feedback on each and every exercise. Candidates become self-evaluators by focusing on their inner self, identify their own strengths and weaknesses, and work upon their weakness by stepping out of their comfort zone to develop till recommended.
